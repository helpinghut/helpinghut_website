Governing Body

At Helping Hut, our mission to make a positive impact on our community is driven by the dedication and passion of our governing body. Our team members, including the Founder & President, Secretary, and Treasurer, play pivotal roles in steering our non-profit organization towards its goals

Together, our governing body forms the backbone of Helping Hut, driving our commitment to making a difference in the lives of those we serve. We invite you to get to know our team better and learn about their contributions to our non-profit's success.
