Empowering Futures: Education Awareness Campaign In Dwarka

October 30 , 2023

EDUCATION AWARENESS CAMPAIGN ,The higher the level of education, the less likely the person is to fall below the poverty line. This is because education imparts skills and knowledge associated with higher wages. Educating girls in particular makes them more likely to take control of decisions relating to fertility, family welfare, and health. HELPING HUT, TEAM DWARKA went down the streets of Dwarka to make people aware about the importance of education on 19th December 2021.education is the best way in which we can contribute to the nation's growth.
