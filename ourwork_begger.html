The notion that "Charity breeds poverty, but investment creates wealth" inspired the creation of the "Beggar Transformation" initiative by Helping Hut NGO. This program seeks to rehabilitate beggars by offering food, counseling, and avenues for employment. In Dwarka, a former tailor turned beggar received assistance from Helping Hut NGO, which provided him with a sewing machine, enabling him to lead a dignified life. The idea behind the initiative is to reform and transform the livelihood and give the beggars dignity and respect, which will be a huge game changer in their lives.
