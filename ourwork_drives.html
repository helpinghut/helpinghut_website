Campaign Drives Recent Work

"Dressing with Dignity: Cloth Distribution Initiatives”

In our efforts to empower lives, we distribute clothing to the underprivileged, offering not just garments but a sense of dignity and pride. Each piece represents a step towards positive change and improved well-being.

In encountering numerous individuals living in impoverished conditions without proper clothing, we recognized the pressing need for support. Providing a pair of clothes to the underprivileged goes beyond addressing a basic necessity—it's about restoring their sense of self-respect. Team Patna, Gwalior, and Dwarka took the initiative to organize cloth distribution campaigns, distributing over 500 clothes donated by our dedicated volunteers and generous contributors. "The Helping Hut" strives to bring a glimmer of hope into the lives of those in need through these efforts.

"Combatting Hunger: Pandemic Relief Food Drive"

"Join us in the fight against hunger. Together, we can make a meaningful impact on the lives of those affected by food insecurity. Your support matters – be a part of the solution and help alleviate this pressing issue in our communities."

"During the pandemic, Helping Hut organized food distribution drives in Patna, New Delhi, and Kota to aid the impoverished. In collaboration with NSS, DDUC, Team New Delhi distributed food packets in Bharat Vihar Kakrola on April 10, 2021. Team Kota reached Khariya Village, Rajasthan, on May 26, 2021, distributing raw food materials. In total, 500+ food packets were distributed by Teams Patna, New Delhi, and Kota. Our volunteers strictly adhered to Covid protocols, wearing masks, carrying sanitizers, and maintaining social distancing in every drive."

“Warmth for All: Blanket Distribution Drive”

Make a difference with our Blanket Distribution Drive, offering warmth to those in need. Your support alleviates poverty by providing essential blankets, offering comfort in the face of cold challenges. Join us in bringing solace and making a positive impact

"Losing yourself in the service of others, as Mahatma Gandhi wisely said, is the path to self-discovery. In the harsh winters, while many of us wrap ourselves in warm layers, there are those with bare bodies, lacking even blankets or shelter. The compassionate volunteers of Helping Hut took a proactive step, extending a helping hand to poverty-stricken individuals by distributing over 100 blankets. In the words of Ronald Reagan, 'We can't help everyone, but everyone can help someone.'"
