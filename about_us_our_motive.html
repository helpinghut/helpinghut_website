Our Vision

“A vision is not just a picture of what could be; it is an appeal to our better selves, a call to become something more” -Rosabeth Moss Kanter"

At HELPING HUT NGO, our vision is to create a more equitable and caring society through the coordination of diverse initiatives, advocacy campaigns, interactive forums, and fundraising efforts. Our goal is to improve the quality of life for those in need and contribute to a more compassionate world.

Our Aim

In our effort to eradicate beggary as a means of livelihood, we aspire to offer alternative occupations that are not only well-suited for individuals but also align with their capabilities and potential

We believe that every child deserves access to education, regardless of their financial circumstances. Our mission is to offer free education to underprivileged children residing in rural areas, ensuring that economic constraints do not hinder their learning opportunities.

Our Mission

Our non-profit organization's core mission is to provide decent housing for all and create a sustainable world for future generations. We strive to ensure that every person in need has the right to a dignified life. Founded by a group of students, our NGO focuses on helping the impoverished, ending beggary through alternative occupations, and providing education to underprivileged children. We achieve our vision by collecting monthly funds from our team and volunteers, using these funds for sustainable campaigns that support our goals.
