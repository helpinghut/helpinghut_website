About The NGO

Helping Hut NGO envisions a beggar-free society where every needy person receives basic living rights. Our mission is to provide employment opportunities to beggars, eradicate begging, and make a positive impact through online campaigns, ground work, and empowering initiatives. Together, we strive to bring joy, awareness, and empowerment to those in need, spreading brightness in their lives and uplifting them from deprivation to knowledge and resources

How We are making the difference

Feed the Striving: Creating Impact with Compassion

In a world where a little love, care, and small acts can bring immense happiness without requiring a fortune, Helping Hut NGO aims to feed the starving and make a positive impact on the lives of those in need.

Empowering Campaigns by Helping Hut NGO

  • Save Water, Plant for Greener Tomorrow: Join our online campaigns to raise awareness about water conservation and its impact on the needy. Be a part of our tree-planting initiative to combat environmental challenges for vulnerable communities
  • Utthan Abhiyan - Empowering the Needy: Explore this impactful campaign that aims to raise awareness about government policies and their benefits for those in need. Be a part of the change and uplift vulnerable communities towards a better future.
  • Artistic Expressions for Change and Quizzes for Knowledge: Unleash your creativity in our competitions focusing on art, craft, and poetry, while engaging in our interactive quizzes that enhance knowledge and understanding, fostering empowerment for a better future.

    • Ground work at Helping Hut brings smiles to the needy through constant efforts, spreading awareness, and distributing masks, education kits, and clothes. We also empower urban and rural children with education, helping them soar to great heights.
